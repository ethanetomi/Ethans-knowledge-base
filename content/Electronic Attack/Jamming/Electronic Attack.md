# Overview
Electronic attack is wide net in the realm of Electromagnetic Warfare and is the subcategory that covers essentially everything that has to do with going on the offensive while utilizing the electromagnetic spectrum. Electronic Attack(EA) can encompass everything from jamming, deception and hijacking. Although we wont be cover the latter much in depth, please refer to the linked pages to find out more information about the aforementioned topics. The purpose of conducting EA is to essentially deprive the adversary of any use of their electrical systems in order to either defend or accomplish a certain mission set. EA is versatile and can be used in virtually any domian or medium needed (Annulli, n.d.).
