# Overview
Electronic attack is wide net in the realm of Electromagnetic Warfare and is the subcategory that covers essentially everything that has to do with going on the offensive while utilizing the electromagnetic spectrum. Electronic Attack(EA) can encompass everything from jamming, deception and hijacking. Although we wont be cover the latter much in depth, please refer to the linked pages to find out more information about the aforementioned topics. The purpose of conducting EA is to essentially deprive the adversary of any use of their electrical systems in order to either defend or accomplish a certain mission set. EA is versatile and can be used in virtually any domain or medium needed (Annulli, n.d.).

## Different types of attack
On the offensive side, EA can be broken down into the following three versions:
* Escort Attack
	* Occurs usually when attack enemy radar for a mission
	* Deals with attaching an manned or unmanned to an aircraft for a flyover mission
* Stand-off Attack
	* Used for either long range attacks on radar or communications
	* Is carried out at a safe distance by an aircraft and is usually only deployable as a stationary unity emitting Ultra High or High frequencies which also means low mobility for the transmitter in order to provide a high level of efficiency and accuracy.
* Stand-in Attack
	* This is aircraft to aircraft / aircraft to air defence systems 
	* Is carried by a risky low flight mission in enemy territory to utilize short range but extremely powerful radio frequencies to directly jamming and completely take down enemy systems.
