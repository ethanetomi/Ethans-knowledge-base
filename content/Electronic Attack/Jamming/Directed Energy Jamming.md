# Overview
Directed energy jamming deals with using directed energy weapons to transmit energy at the speed of light with the purpose of inflicting damage onto a targeted electronic system (Sentrycs, n.d.). Often times used on other jammers, unmanned aircraft systems(UAS), and radars.

## Why DEJ is important
It offers a kill-all mode of attack against adversary methods if previous more convential methods were to fail. Due to the complete frying and destructive outcome of the targeted electronic system, DEJ is often looked at as one of the last resorts or backup plans this way ensures that there or minimal or none unintended consequences of using the technology. Essentially any electronics within the targeted area (people as well) can be affected. So for things like UAS being operated near a security stronghold would need be taken down completely and swiftly before the adversary attack can be carried out, that is where DEJ comes in. Although there are other more controlled cyber methods that are game planned to be first in utilization, if those were to fail, DEJ serves as the final line of defense as catchall (Sentrycs, n.d.).
